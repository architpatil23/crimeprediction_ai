WHAT WE DO

Know the diverse, helpful, user-friendly, and most importantly, accurate range of features we offer.

View Crime Statistics

Currently only for law enforcement agencies, we offer the LEAs a platform to map and digitally store the data on a secure server. More the data, more the accuracy in our results. Once you login, use the Add Crime Instance button to log the crime in the database.

Crime Prediction

Being our USP, this is the most important feature that we provide. Post the processing and cleaning of the data provided, we allow you to predict the probability of a crime happening on the basis of only four parameters: Neighborhood Area, District, Hour of Crime, Day of Crime

Upload Crime Dataset

Currently only for law enforcement agencies, we offer the LEAs a platform to map and digitally store the data on a secure server. More the data, more the accuracy in our results. Further, we train our Deep Learning model to make a prediction on the basis of the dataset uploaded, hence ensuring accuracy and practicability.
